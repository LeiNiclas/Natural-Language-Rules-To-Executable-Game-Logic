Play rules Tic-Tac-Toe
* For 2 players
Each player plays with 5 identical pieces.
They are placed one after one
on the playing field.
The first player to get 3 of his marks in a row
(up, down, across or diagonally)
is the winner.
When all 9 squares are full, the game is over.
