The rules of Nim
The traditional game of Nim is played with a number of counters arranged in heaps:
the number of counters and heaps is as follows: 3 Heaps with 10, 6, 3 coins.
There are two players. When it's a player's move he or she can take any number of coins from a single heap.
They have to take at least one coin, though, and they can't take coins from more than one heap.
The winner is the player who makes the last move, so there are no coins left after that move.
